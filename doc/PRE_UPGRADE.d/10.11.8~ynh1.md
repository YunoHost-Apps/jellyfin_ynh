This package upgrades Jellyfin to latest 10.11.8 release, which includes security fixes published in 10.11.7.

It is also a packaging update, where the installation and data directories are moved to meet the standard YunoHost apps directories (installation in `/var/www/jellyfin` and data in `/home/yunohost.app/jellyfin`).
The backup/restore procedure should be more robust as the Jellyfin assets don’t have to be downloaded again on restore (everything is in the backup).
You also now can use a second instance of Jellyfin on your server.
